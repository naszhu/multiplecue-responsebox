PRACTICE 1

In the following, you will see a black point in the center of the screen. Please always fixate this point when you start a trial. 

Upon pressing *space*, a colored circle with a digit from 1 to 4 will appear in the center of the screen.

__________

Your task is report the color of the circle by pressing one of four keys: "Z", "X", ".", and "-".

The keys are associated with four colors: RED, GREEN, BLUE, and YELLOW. 

The order of the colors relative to the keys are shown below the circle.

__________

You will earn points by reporting the color of the circle correctly while also responding as quickly as possible.

The digit 1 allows you to earn 1 point, and so on until a maximum reward of 4 points per trial. 

Your collected points will be translated into a monetary reward that is added to your compensation payment. After each trial, you will see how many points you have earned in that trial relative to what you could have earned, along with the amount of money in DKK that you have obtained. 
You will also see the speed of your responses. Please, try to be as fast as possible while still earning as many points as possible.
__________

Remember to fixate the black point in the center at the start of each trial.  

Press *space* to begin.
